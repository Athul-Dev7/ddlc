The Shift

A paradigm shift in control engineering

Unlike traditional control systems that rely strictly on fixed logic rules, DDLCs leverage the power of continuous data streams and artificial intelligence.

Sitting invisibly atop Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs), DDLCs act as intelligent orchestrators. They constantly analyze vast amounts of data from multiple structural sources, dynamically overriding and optimizing system behavior instantly to prevent faults.

Core Capabilities

Engineered for extreme adaptability

Data Agnostic Integration

DDLCs are highly versatile integration layers. They effortlessly process a wide spectrum of data types simultaneously, including raw sensor readings, high-frame-rate images, spatial audio, and localized structured plant data.

Real-Time AI Inference

By pushing machine learning to the deep edge, DDLCs learn from real-world physical anomalies. They execute informed operational overrides in real-time, boosting yield quality and preventing mechanical downtime.

Multi-Modal ML Engine

Standard logic looks at one sensor. Our Multi-Modal engine offers a holistic view of mechanical dynamics by evaluating sight, sound, and pressure inputs concurrently, forming a complete intelligence net.
